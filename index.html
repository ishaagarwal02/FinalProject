Endowments at Public vs. Private Colleges

As you explore what state you might want to go to school in, it's important to consider endowment* sizes for each college. Endowments fund scholarships and give financial stability to each school. Private institutions tend to have larger endowments, but this isn't always the case. See whether your desired state has any exceptions by using the dropdown menu below, and hover over the bubbles to see the individual endowments!
